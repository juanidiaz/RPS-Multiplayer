Multiplayer edition

Welcome to our on-line version of the popular rock-paper-scissors game. In this version you will be able to play the game or to just "watch" other people play. Or, if a spot is available, you can play the game against someone else.

Instructions

  • Click the VIEWER button if you want don't want to play and just want to follow other's game.
  • Once the draw is done you can keep playing or quit and become a viewer... thus leving the player spot empty.
  • Make sure you don't close or refresh your browser as this will END YOUR GAME!
